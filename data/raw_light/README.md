# Lightweight Alternative Raw Data

Raw alternative datasets for Asia, May 2025-May 2026.

Downloaded:

- `era5_monthly/`: Copernicus ERA5 monthly averaged single-levels for 2m temperature, 2m dewpoint temperature, and total precipitation over the Asia bounding box.
- `mod11c3_lst/`: NASA MODIS MOD11C3 v061 monthly global CMG land surface temperature files for May 2025-April 2026. May 2026 is not published yet.
- `mcd12c1_landcover/`: NASA MCD12C1 v061 yearly global land-cover CMG for 2024. The 2025 yearly product is not published yet.
- `gmted2010/`: USGS GMTED2010 30 arc-second global mean elevation grid.
- `download_manifest_light_alternatives_2025_05_to_2026_05.json`: exact paths, statuses, and missing/blocked items.

Not downloaded:

- GPM IMERG monthly precipitation is blocked until the Earthdata account accepts the GES DISC data EULA/application authorization. ERA5 monthly total precipitation is included as the rainfall variable.
